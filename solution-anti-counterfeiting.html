Three Layers: One Framework, No Gaps

Why single-method protection always fails.

A QR code can be copied. A hologram can be faked. A forensic tag requires a lab. We combined all three into a single scan, so a counterfeiter has to beat a physical barrier, a digital barrier, and an invisible barrier—simultaneously.

Visual verification without a device.

Consumers shouldn't have to download an app to know if they're buying a fake. The Nano-OVD (Optically Variable Device) label provides instant, physical proof of authenticity. Unlike standard holographic stickers sourced from commercial printers, our nano-lithography is controlled, tracked, and serialized.

Catch the copies you can't see.

If a counterfeiter copies a legitimate QR code and prints it on 10,000 fake boxes, visual inspection won't catch it. Our covert layer does. If a unique code is scanned in Mumbai on Tuesday and Nairobi on Wednesday, the system flags the anomaly, blacklists the code, and alerts your brand protection team with the exact GPS coordinates.

Legally admissible proof.

When you need to take action against a grey-market distributor or defend against an unjustified liability claim, you need non-repudiable proof. We embed a forensic, AI-generated watermark directly into the packaging artwork or the label grain. It is entirely invisible to the naked eye but acts as a definitive biometric fingerprint under authorized scanning.
