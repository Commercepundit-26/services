CLIENT PROFILE

Global Agrochemical Manufacturer

A Fortune 500 chemical and crop protection manufacturer supplying high-value formulations to distributors, retailers, and commercial farms in over 10 countries.

• Scale: 320M+ annual product units • ERP Stack: SAP S/4HANA Enterprise Suite • Deployment: 14 manufacturing plants across 3 continents
THE CHALLENGE

Widespread Clones & Supply Chain Blind Spots

Sophisticated counterfeiters were copying single-layer QR labels on high-value chemical SKUs. Duplicate products entered regional distributor networks unnoticed, leading to crop failure complaints, brand liability, and millions in lost revenue.

Furthermore, existing ERP modules couldn't track individual bottle identity beyond the plant warehouse door.

THE SMART EPSILON SOLUTION

End-to-End Serialization & 3-Layer Protection

Unit-Level Serialization

Unique GS1 EPCIS 2.0 digital identity created at the packaging line, permanently tying batch, line, and shift data to each bottle.

3-Layer Security Markers

Overt nano-OVD holographic labels combined with covert geo-tagged clone detection and invisible AI watermark authentication.

Real-Time Clone Alerts

Immediate geolocation alerts sent to field teams whenever a duplicate code is scanned outside its expected market node.
